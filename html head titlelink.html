Ilhia College of Engineering And Technology

Ilahia College of Engineering and Technology (ICET) has varying placement performance reports; one source indicates 30-50% placements with an average monthly salary around ₹15,000-₹25,000 and top recruiters including IBM, Tech Mahindra, and Wipro, while other promotional material from 2019 and more recent social media posts highlight 100% placement support or over 150 students placed with top MNCs for the 2019 batch.
